# Campaign Decision Record Format

Campaign decision records live in `docs/campaign-decisions/` and use sequential numbering:

```text
0001-phoenix-heart-as-propaganda.md
0002-magi-rationalism-over-religion.md
```

## Template

```md
# {Short title of the campaign decision}

{1-3 sentences: what was decided, why it matters, and what trade-off it resolves.}
```

## Optional Sections

Use only when valuable:

- **Status**: proposed | accepted | deprecated | superseded by CDR-NNNN
- **Considered Options**
- **Consequences**
- **Affected Files**
- **Player-Facing Implications**

## When to Create One

Create a CDR only when all three are true:

1. Hard to reverse across campaign prep
2. Surprising without context
3. Result of a meaningful trade-off

## What Qualifies

- Core cosmology decisions
- Hidden-truth decisions
- Major faction agenda decisions
- Structural campaign decisions
- Retcons or continuity repairs
- Player knowledge boundary decisions

## What Does Not Qualify

- Local color
- One-off NPC details
- Monster substitutions
- Tactical encounter tuning
- Names unless they encode major canon
