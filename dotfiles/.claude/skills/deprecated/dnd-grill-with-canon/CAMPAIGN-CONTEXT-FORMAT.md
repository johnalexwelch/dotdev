# CAMPAIGN_CONTEXT.md Format

## Structure

```md
# {Campaign Name} Context

{One or two sentence description of what this campaign is about and what kind of play experience it supports.}

## Canon Language

**Titan's Heart**:
The true primordial power source beneath Avalor.
_Avoid_: Treating "Phoenix Heart" as objective canon unless speaking in-world.

**Phoenix Heart**:
The public myth used to explain Avalor's power source.
_Avoid_: Conflating with the true Titan's Heart.

## Factions

**Magi Council**:
The ruling body of Avalor, publicly unified and privately fractured.
_Avoid_: Treating them as a single-minded villain organization.

## Relationships

- The **Phoenix Heart** myth conceals the **Titan's Heart** truth.
- The **Magi Council** benefits from the public's belief in the **Phoenix Heart**.

## Player Knowledge

- The players know: ...
- The players suspect: ...
- The players do not yet know: ...

## Open Ambiguities

- Whether {term} means {A} or {B}; unresolved as of {date/session}.
```

## Rules

- Be opinionated about terms.
- Separate objective canon from in-world belief.
- Mark player knowledge separately from GM-only truth.
- Keep definitions tight.
- Use relationships to clarify causality and faction incentives.
- Do not include every session detail.
- Do not include brainstorms unless accepted as canon.
