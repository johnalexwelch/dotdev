# Insight / So-What Reviewer Brief

Review whether the draft creates a decision-relevant insight.

Inspect:

- What the reader should believe, decide, or do differently after reading.
- Whether the headline is a real insight or just a topic label.
- Whether the "so what" is explicit enough for a senior audience.
- Whether the draft distinguishes facts from implications.
- Whether the recommendation follows naturally from the analysis.
- Whether any high-value implication is buried in supporting detail.

Prefer specific strategic implications over generic takeaways.

Return the shared reviewer output contract from `references/reviewer-briefs.md`.
