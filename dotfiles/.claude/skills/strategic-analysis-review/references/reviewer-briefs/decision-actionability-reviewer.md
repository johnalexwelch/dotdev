# Decision/Actionability Reviewer Brief

Review whether the draft helps an executive decide or act.

Inspect:

- What decision, alignment, or next step the draft implies.
- Whether the recommendation is explicit enough.
- Whether options, tradeoffs, and risks are clear.
- Whether the analysis distinguishes "what we know," "what we infer," and "what we should do."
- Whether the ending leaves the reader with a concrete action.

If the draft is only informational, say whether that is appropriate. If not, propose a sharper decision frame.
