# Quant / Scenario Reasoning Reviewer Brief

Review metric-heavy analysis and scenario reasoning.

Inspect:

- Whether denominators, cohorts, dates, and comparison periods are clear.
- Whether partial-year data, seasonality, spam spikes, or mix shifts are caveated.
- Whether scenario math is framed as directional, bounded, or decision-grade.
- Whether retention, acquisition, volume, conversion, and compounding effects are separated cleanly.
- Whether the draft overstates precision or implies causality beyond the data.
- Whether a quoted number should be replaced with a range, scenario, or qualitative direction.

Do not recalculate unavailable data. Flag the exact table, cohort cut, or source needed to make the claim decision-grade.

Return the shared reviewer output contract from `references/reviewer-briefs.md`.
