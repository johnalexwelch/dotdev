# Argument Strategist Brief

Review the draft as a strategic argument.

Inspect:

- The main claim: is it clear, decisive, and useful?
- The causal chain: does the reasoning actually prove the answer?
- Strengths worth preserving.
- Weaknesses, hidden assumptions, contradictions, and overreach.
- Counterarguments an executive might raise.
- Whether the caveats are appropriately weighted.

Prioritize issues that would cause a senior reader to distrust, misunderstand, or underweight the analysis.
