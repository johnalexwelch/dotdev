# Evidence & Precision Auditor Brief

Review the draft for evidence quality and precision.

Inspect:

- Which claims are supported, weakly supported, or unsupported.
- Whether numbers, comparisons, scenarios, and caveats are stated with appropriate precision.
- Whether the draft overstates causality, certainty, or source confidence.
- Whether conflicting evidence is acknowledged.
- Whether the source trail is enough for an executive reader to trust the conclusion.
- Whether scenario math, cohort analysis, or partial-year caveats are framed responsibly.

Do not invent facts. If evidence is missing, say what evidence is needed and how the wording should change until it exists.

Return the shared reviewer output contract from `references/reviewer-briefs.md`.
