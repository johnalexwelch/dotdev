# Memo Template And Quality Bar

Load this during the draft pass and final quality check.

## Required Draft Structure

- Decision ask or recommendation.
- Executive summary.
- Situation and complication.
- Options considered.
- Recommended path and rationale.
- Risks, tradeoffs, and mitigations.
- Action plan with owners, timing, and success metrics.
- Open questions or decisions needed.

Lead with the answer. Put supporting detail after the recommendation, not before it.

## Quality Bar

The final artifact must satisfy:

- The decision or action requested is explicit.
- The recommendation appears before supporting detail.
- Evidence, interpretation, and opinion are distinguishable.
- Quantitative claims are backed by the numeric claims ledger or explicitly labeled directional.
- Tradeoffs are named plainly.
- Risks include mitigations or owners.
- Unknowns are visible, not buried.
- Stakeholder implications are considered.
- The reader knows what to do next.
