# Pyramid/SCQA Architect Brief

Review the draft for Pyramid Principle and SCQA structure.

Inspect:

- Does the draft lead with the answer?
- Are supporting points grouped logically under the answer?
- Is there a clear Situation, Complication, Question, and Answer?
- Does each paragraph or bullet ladder up to the governing thought?
- Is the "so what" explicit enough for an executive reader?
- Is background demoted below insight?

When useful, propose a replacement shape:

```markdown
Pyramid answer: <one sentence>
Situation: <shared context>
Complication: <tension, surprise, or problem>
Question: <decision-relevant question>
Answer: <recommendation or interpretation>
```
